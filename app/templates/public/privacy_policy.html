Privacy Policy — {{ game.name }}

Effective date: {{ effective_date.strftime('%B %-d, %Y') if effective_date else '—' }}

This Privacy Policy explains how {{ operator }} ("we", "us") collects, uses, and shares information in connection with the leaderboard for {{ game.name }} (the "Game"). It applies specifically to the leaderboard service for this Game. By submitting a score, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information we collect

When you submit a score, we collect the following:

  • Player name. The display name you choose to attach to your score. Please do not use your real name or any other personal information — the name is shown publicly on the leaderboard.
  • Gameplay data. Your score, the time the score was achieved and submitted, the daily-challenge "seed" (if applicable), and any game-specific fields the Game chooses to send (for example, level reached or combo count).
  • Device and technical information. Information about the device and software you use to submit a score, which may include operating system, browser or app identifier, device model, application/game version, screen resolution, locale, and platform. Some of this is derived from your browser/app's User-Agent string; some may be provided by the Game.
  • IP address. Your IP address is processed temporarily to enforce rate limits, prevent abuse, and protect the service. It is not stored alongside your score in our leaderboard database.

2. How we use information

  • To display public leaderboards and daily-challenge "champion" tallies.
  • To operate, maintain, and secure the service.
  • To detect, prevent, and respond to cheating, fraud, and abuse.
  • To compute aggregate, non-identifying statistics (such as win counts per player name).

3. How we share information

  • Public display. Leaderboard entries — including the player name, score, and a summary of device/OS information — are publicly visible through the Game and the service's public API.
  • No sale of data. We do not sell your information.
  • Third parties. This is a self-hosted service. We do not share your information with third-party advertisers or analytics providers as part of its default operation. We may disclose information if required to do so by law, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of the service and its users.
  • Service providers. Information is stored on the infrastructure operated by {{ operator }} and any hosting provider they use to run this service.

4. Cookies

Submitting and viewing scores does not require or set any cookies. A single cookie is used only to keep administrators signed in to the private admin area; it is not used for players or for tracking.

5. Data retention

Scores and their associated data are retained until removed by an administrator or upon a valid request (see "Your choices" below). Administrators may hide or delete scores at their discretion (for example, to remove cheated entries). Technical data used for rate limiting (such as your IP address) is short-lived and expires automatically.

6. Your choices and rights

Leaderboard entries are pseudonymous — they are tied only to the player name you chose, not to an account. Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete information that relates to you. To request the removal or correction of a score, contact us using the details in the "Contact" section below and include enough information (such as the player name, Game, and approximate score and date) to identify the entry.

7. Children's privacy

This service is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. Players should not submit personal information as a player name. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will take reasonable steps to remove it.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information, including hashing administrator credentials and supporting encrypted (HTTPS) connections. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. International users

Your information is processed and stored on infrastructure operated by {{ operator }}, which may be located in a different country than your own. By using the service, you understand that your information may be transferred to and processed in those locations.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Effective date" above. Continued use of the leaderboard after a change indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
